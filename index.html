Who We Are

Overview

Etihad Group is a privately owned prominent conglomerate formed through a strategic partnership between Chaudhry Shahzad and Rana Iftikar Ahmad, two visionary leaders with a combined wealth of experience and expertise.

Eithad Group is the most progressive business group in worldwide, The Group has made significant investment in a wide array of sectors, including: Energy Sector, Manufacturing and Processing, Logistics and Transportation.

Beyond our origins, Etihad Group has expanded its footprint internationally, establishing operations in key markets such as the United States, United Kingdom, United Arab Emirates, Pakistan, Saudi Arabia, and other GCC countries.

Etihad Group is committed to fostering innovation and sustainability across all sectors. Our initiatives aim not only to achieve business success but also to make a positive impact on the communities we serve.

Through the synergy of our partnership, driving growth. We are dedicated to maintaining our leadership position in diversified industries while pursuing continuous innovation and making a meaningful societal impact.
